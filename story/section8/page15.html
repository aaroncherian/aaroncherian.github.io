Section 8

Lilah threw out her hands. Tom reacted quickly, jumping away at her movement. He felt an intense pain along the side of his body like no other as the fire razed him and nearly dropped the gun. Flames began to circle him, blocking his vision. All the same he held steady, and squeezed the trigger. Three times. Three shots rang out.

The fire in front of him vanished into glowing purple embers that quickly faded into nothing. He looked down at the floor in front of him. Lilah was on her back, blood pooling around her body. She was looking up, her fingers twitching slightly. But slowly she turned her head to Tom. She moved her mouth slightly, but there was no sound.

Tom slumped to the ground and leaned back against the bench. He sat there for a few minutes, listening to her pained breaths as they became quieter and quieter. When he looked over at her next, her sightless eyes were still staring at him.

Tom shut his eyes.
