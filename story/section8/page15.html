Section 8

Lilah threw out her hands, and the fire razed him along the side. He felt an intense pain in his arm like no other and nearly dropped the gun. But he held steady, and squeezed the trigger. Three times. Three shots rang out.

The fire disappeared around him and the bar. There were two bodies in front of him now, one with blood slowly pooling around it.

Tom shut his eyes.
