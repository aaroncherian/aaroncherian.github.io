Section 8

Tom started speaking out in protest to tell Enzo that no, none of this was true at all, because Enzo would believe him as always when –Crack!- a bolt of electricity so bright that Tom had to shut his eyes shot past Lilah. Her eyes grew wide for a second as she shifted slightly towards the side as she lost her balance.

“Don’t lie to me,” Enzo said in a deceptively calm tone.

“Think about it,” Lilah urged him, a note of panic entering her voice for the first time. “Ayano had been getting to scenes faster than most other heroes, even when she wasn’t on-shift, and Tom didn’t like that. And she didn’t care for the new commission system, no, she didn’t care about the money at all, but Tom did. The other leaders and captains did. And so-“
