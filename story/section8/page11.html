Section 8

Fire surged towards Enzo the moment he turned his back on Lilah. In a matter of seconds raging flames circled around him. Enzo spun back around, unleashing bright sparks of electricity that lit up the air as he did, but the flames twisted into the space beneath his feet and erupted upwards in a roaring pillar of fire around him. Tom looked on in horror as Enzo was burned alive by the inferno in front of him.
