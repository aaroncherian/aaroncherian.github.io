Section 8

Everything seemed to freeze for a few seconds, and all Tom heard was the crackling of fire and lightning as they danced around each other. Enzo seemed to take a few deep breaths, and when he spoke his words came slow and weighted,

“We already…know,” he grumbled. “Gravitas.”

“Sure, that’s who killed her in the end,” Lilah said as amiably as if they had agreed on the weather outside. “But who’s actually responsible? See in part it’s you and me, Enzo. Bryan too, but he realized the truth at the very end. We were all too blind to see it.”
