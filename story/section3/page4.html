Section 3

Tom tried to interject, but found his facial muscles unresponsive, his own tongue frozen in his mouth.

He felt the cold this time like a frigid wind blowing over him. He struggled, even though he knew it was futile. He needed to talk to Lilah, to Enzo. Even a one-sided conversation with Bryan would be preferable to living through another memory.

He blinked, and the bar was empty, the teammates he knew vanished from sight.

A second time and he was back in costume again. Dusty dim lightbulbs suddenly became clean and bright.

He blinked a third. There was a numbness as he lost any remaining control over his body.

Tom thought back to the past, but everything past the night Ayano died was still a haze.

Fine, he decided. Show me a memory with an answer this time, and I'll play along with whatever this is.
