Section 3

The meaning of her words took a few moments to register completely for Tom. He realized that Enzo had started talking again.

“I just keep thinking that if we had stopped her from leaving that night, she would still be alive.”

Tom felt something at his core go cold, and the pounding in his head increased tenfold.

“Come on Enzo, we’ve been through this, nothing could have stopped Ayano from going where someone was in need. We … I … someone should’ve gone with her instead, maybe she would still be here,” Lilah said. “But why would we ever think she would need back up? She was the fastest woman in the city, and we thought it was just a simple mugging. Who ever thought anyone could touch her, let alone kill her?”
