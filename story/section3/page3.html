Section 3

Kill her.

The memory slammed through him like a thunderbolt as he remembered the rest of that night with startling clarity.

They were about to leave the bar for the night when they heard the news. The Scarlet Speedstress was dead.

She had been trapped and murdered in an alleyway, her body abandoned on the frozen ground until an unfortunate passerby had come across it.

How could he have forgotten? How could he have let himself believe that she had been alive and with them just a few minutes ago?
