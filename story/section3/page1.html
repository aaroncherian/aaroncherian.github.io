Section 3

Tom turned his attention towards the others, where Bryan, Enzo, and Lilah seemed unaware of what had just occurred. He frowned, Ayano’s seat was empty. Where had she gone?

Enzo looked over at him.

“We’re making the toast,” he said, taking one of four glasses that must have arrived while Tom was otherwise distracted.

A toast? Odd that they weren’t waiting for Ayano to come back.

Tom picked up a glass nevertheless.

“To a reunion a long time coming.” Lilah said.

Tom looked over to where Ayano had been sitting again, realizing her coat was gone as well. When had she left?

“And to those who couldn’t be here with us tonight,” Enzo said, choking up slightly.

Something in Tom’s head started pounding, and he winced at the pain slightly.

“To Ayano, our Scarlet Speedstress. We miss you.”

Perhaps a touch overemotional, Tom mused, considering Ayano had been there for most of the night, but also perfectly within Enzo’s tendency towards over-exaggeration.

Lilah and Bryan raised their glasses.

“Seven years to the day,” Lilah said. “You served our city well.”
