Section 9

“Leaving already, Tom?”

Ayano sat at the table looking back at him.

Tom nodded.

“It’s time, Ayano. I’m so sorry, but it’s about time I leave,” he said.

Ayano took a moment before nodding as well.

“I understand, Tom.”

Tom turned around and opened the door.

“But you’ll be back tomorrow, right?” Ayano called out.

When Tom turned back he saw Bryan, Enzo and Lilah waving to him from the table as well.
