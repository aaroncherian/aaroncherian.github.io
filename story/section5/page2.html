Section 5

“I had thought there was a pattern. That I was just dozing off at our reunion and I would wake up between memories,” Tom responded, his voice quiet at first, but firmer as he spoke. “But then there was this, talking to you, someone that doesn’t exist in a place that doesn’t exist. Which means that either there is no pattern or that the pattern is something else. Every time I finish a memory, I don’t actually wake up do I? It’s just another hallucination, something to keep me occupied before the next memory.”

Lilah nodded rapidly, her face lighting up.

“This is good, this is really good Tom. I’ll be able to use this next time. Even if you don’t remember, I will. We’ll be that much closer to breaking out.”

Tom tried to respond, but he found himself frozen.
