Section 5

Lilah gave him a sympathetic look.

“I know, and I’m sorry I can’t stop it, but we’ve run out of time, Tom. I can at least take you to the next memory.”

She waved. There was no physical feeling of coldness this time as Tom fell into the next memory, only an icy feeling in his gut.

The bar was full again, but at their table there were only three of them. Bryan sat next to Lilah, while Tom sat alone on the other side of the table, all of them dressed in costume. Time was frozen around them.

“This is around the fall after she died,” Lilah explained. “We had just caught Ayano’s killer, a man named Gravitas. He was an old enemy of her mother’s. Someone with a vendetta. We found him, brought him in. You convinced Enzo that to join Shard on that job downtown.

“And Tom,” she said, pausing a moment. “Good luck.”

She closed her eyes and the memory around them resumed.
