Section 4

Bryan rose up slowly after Enzo.

“Going back to base?” Lilah asked. Bryan nodded. Planning to interrogate more suspects, no doubt. Lilah smiled and held out a closed fist.

“Give ‘em hell Bry,” she said. Bryan returned the fist bump with a smile of his own before leaving the two of them alone at the table.

Lilah avoided looking directly at Tom, but he didn't miss how her smile faded as Bryan walked away.

“He’s a good guy, you know.”

Those were the first direct words she had spoken to him in a while. Lilah started to gather together her belongings.

“You can pretend that you’re worried about him Tom, but I know what you’re really worried about. You don’t want him to find out about what we did.”

“And you do?” Tom asked. “You want him to find out about us because he accidently reads one of our minds?”
