Section 4

Bryan rose up slowly after Enzo.

“Are you going back to Headquarters?” Lilah asked. Bryan nodded, and Tom knew that he planned to interrogate more suspects. Lilah smiled and held out a closed fist.

“Give ‘em hell Bryan,” she said. Bryan returned the fist bump with a smile of his own before leaving the two of them alone at the table.

Lilah’s smile faded as he left.

“He’s a good guy, you know.” Tom looked up in surprise. Those were the first direct words she had spoken to him in a while. She started to gather together her belongings, as she continued.

“You can pretend that you’re worried about him, Tom, but I know what you’re really worried about. You don’t want him to find out about what we did.”

“And you do?” Tom asked. “You want him to find out about us because he accidently reads one of our minds?”
