Section 4

As Lilah began to walk away, Tom felt an intense chill wash over him again. He shivered as ice began to crawl through his veins. He knew the pattern at this point however.

He blinked once, and everyone disappeared.

Twice and his costume was switched for his civilian clothing.

A third and-

“Just a second, Tom. We still need to talk about a few things.”

Lilah stood just near the doorway, her back to Tom as she spoke. Apart from the two of them the bar was completely empty. She waved a hand, and Tom found himself able to move freely.
