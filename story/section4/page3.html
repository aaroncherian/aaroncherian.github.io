Section 4

Bryan’s gaze sharpened as he looked at Tom, determination and resolve filling them, but not quite managing to wipe the tiredness from his face.

Letters arranged themselves on the table in front of Tom.

I’m fine , they read.

Tom looked to Lilah for support, but she pointedly ignored his gaze. He looked back to Enzo,

“You need distance,” he said. “Shard’s forming a temporary team for a big job downtown, linking up with local law enforcement. She’ll need some heavy hitters, and I can recommend you.”

Enzo started to protest, but Tom cut him off.

“Do you think this is what Ayano would have wanted for us? Ignoring people in need to help track down her killers?”
