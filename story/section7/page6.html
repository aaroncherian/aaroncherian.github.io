Section 7

Tom blinked, and when he opened his eyes, he was in costume, and the bar was alive again.

Enzo was still the only occupant of the table.

“When are we?” Tom asked.

“About a year from the time Ayano died. A few months after Bryan died. And Lilah, well she went off the grid for a bit. Neither of us had seen or heard from her since Bryan’s death. She’ll be here soon enough.” Enzo responded.

A thought occurred to Tom.

“Enzo, I never asked. What happened to you- the real you?”

Enzo only had time to give Tom a sad smile before the door opened.
