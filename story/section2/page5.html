Section 2

He looked up at Bryan, who looked down pointedly at the space in front of Tom in return.

Tom followed his gaze down, and saw the image of inky black letters coming together on the table. It was Bryan’s way of communicating something too abstract for images. It was slow and clunky, but worked well enough when they had time to idle around.

“Give her some time, Tom. She’ll learn. ” It read. The letters were written in a perfect Comic Sans font. Bryan had spent days meticulously memorizing and perfecting every curve and letter to imitate the font, and Tom didn’t quite understand why, but Bryan and Enzo found the entire concept uproariously funny.

Tom felt numb for a moment, before panic started pump through his systems.
