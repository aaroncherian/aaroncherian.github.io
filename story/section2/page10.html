Bryan returned a few seconds afterwards, sliding back into the seat, looking around curiously, noting everyone’s silence. He seemed to be asking for an explanation but Lilah shook her head slightly, and he settled into their table. The next twenty minutes were largely punctuated by brief spurts of conversation that soon faded away, until a sudden bright, cheerful ringtone rang out.

They all simultaneously turned towards Ayano, who reached for her pocket. Tom noticed that around the bar the phones of a few others did the same, but their owners quickly silenced them. Ayano’s brow furrowed as she listened,

She stood up and stowed the phone away. She almost ran out the door then before realizing that the rest of them were looking at her expectantly.
