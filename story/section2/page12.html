Section 2

Ayano barely gave him a glance as she disappeared, and Tom heard the door of the bar open and shut.

Everything seemed to shift and spin around Tom. He closed his eyes to ward off the dizziness, and opened them again to the present. He found himself able to move and speak freely, and twisted to the side. His reflection, an older, somber man stared back impassively at him through the window pane. Tom blinked and then looked away. The memory of that night was there now, and yet had done little to clear up his confusion.
