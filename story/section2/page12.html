"Someone just called in, said she saw a man being attacked in an alleyway. She reported that the attacker was using powers. Real close by, just a few blocks away. I’ll take care of it quickly.”

“Ayano, listen to me,” Tom said. “There are plenty of other heroes, go if you’d like, but remember, you are not on patrol. ”

Ayano barely gave him a glance as she disappeared in a crimson blur, and Tom heard the door of the bar open and shut.
