Section 1

“Ayano!” He stood up and called out, waving vigorously. There was a sudden zap, and the lights above them and the tables adjacent to them blinked. Electricity flickered into existence for a moment around Enzo’s hand before dispersing.

Tom couldn’t help but flinch as blue sparks arced dangerously in his direction.

“Every time,” He muttered, taking a sip from his drink.

Chatter diminished for a few seconds, but quickly resumed throughout the bar. Ayano smiled widely and then disappeared into a faintly red blur that made its way towards the table in less than a few seconds.
