Section 1

“That long huh?” Lilah said quietly, before smiling and looking over at Tom. “Well thanks for setting this whole thing up Tom, I think I can speak for everyone when I say it’s good to be back. The place is still as loud as ever. And chaotic.”

She glanced over at Enzo as she said that last part. “Although from what I’ve heard not everyone from our days of being on patrol made it.”

Ayano shook her head solemnly.

“We’ve lost some good people.”

“They served our city well,” Tom murmured. The others repeated the phrase after him, as had long been the custom of honoring fallen heroes.
