Section 1

“Finally!” Enzo exclaimed, slamming a fist down onto the table. “Do you know how bored I was getting waiting for the food?”

There was a crackle as his fist struck the polished wood, and finally a zap!

Electricity flickered into existence around his hand before dispersing.

“Enzo!” Tom growled as a few sparks arced dangerously in his direction.

Bryan stifled a laugh.

Enzo shrugged, unapologetic, before proceeding to stab at his food with a knife.

Tom could have in fact summed up the entirety of the man’s existence as unapologetic. Neatly styled dark hair, except for a few loose strands hanging over his forehead, easily fixable with a comb if Enzo so desired to. He didn’t.

An otherwise pressed and neat shirt, but for a mustard yellow stain along the forearm that could Enzo could have hidden by rolling his sleeves up, if he so desired. He didn’t.

“Incorrigible as always. Glad to see you haven’t changed, Lorenzo,” Lilah said.

Enzo grinned for a brief moment, before his smile faded.
