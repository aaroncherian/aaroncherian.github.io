Section 1

Ding

His attention was drawn by the sound of a small chime ringing out as the door opened. A woman wearing a brilliant red coat strolled in, frigid air blowing in behind her.

She brushed powdery snow off her sleeves and shoulders, unperturbed by the small slivers of liquid water that flowed off of her and towards the hero in blue. She nodded her thanks and turned towards the back off the bar, her eyes perusing the tables as she sought them out.

Ordinarily she might have stood there searching a while longer. The bar was particularly full that night, and the colorful costumes worn by its super-powered clientele might have drawn her gaze away from where they sat in civilian clothing, at a table towards the back of the bar and up a short flight of stairs, had Enzo not taken it upon himself to draw her attention.
