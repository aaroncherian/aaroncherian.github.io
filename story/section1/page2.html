Section 1

“Can I help you?” Tom asked, mildly exasperated.

“I think she’s here!” Enzo said excitedly, gesturing towards the door. Tom sat up and looked across the bar. The other two occupants of their table, a man and woman sitting across from him, did the same. The woman frowned as they waited for the door to open.

“Now Enzo, did you actually see her or are you just guessing as usual?” She asked. Enzo laughed,

“Look Lilah, I know none of us would actually see her coming, she’s the fastest woman in the city. But I thought I saw a streak of red go past all the windows and Bryan I can see you rolling your eyes at me and I don't quite understand why.” The last statement was directed towards the man sitting next to Lilah.

"Because that's exactly what you said a few minutes ago!” Lilah said, sharing a glance with Bryan. As Enzo and Lilah began their usual course of bickering, Tom found his attention being drawn to the bar as a whole.
