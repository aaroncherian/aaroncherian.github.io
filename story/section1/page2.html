Section 1

Sharp pokes to his side suddenly snapped him out of his stupor. Tom turned to the man sitting next to him.

“Can I help you?” He asked, only mildly exasperated. The man grinned unapologetically and gestured towards direction of the bar’s door.

“I think she’s here!” he said excitedly. Tom sat up a little straighter and looked across the bar. Across from him, another man and woman did the same. The woman frowned as they waited for the door to open.

“Enzo, we’re talking about the woman with super-speed, would you really be able to tell she was here?” She questioned. Enzo raised a finger in response,

“Ah, well you see Lilah, I’m fairly certain I saw her tell-tale crimson streak go past all the windows-“he started. Tom’s attention to Enzo waned as he continued speaking, and he instead found himself looking out at the bar as a whole.
