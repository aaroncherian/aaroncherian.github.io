Section 1

“Can I help you?” He asked, mildly exasperated. The man grinned unapologetically and gestured towards the door.

“I think she’s here!” he said excitedly. Tom sat up a little straighter and looked across the bar. The other occupants of the table, a man and woman sitting across from him, did the same. The woman frowned as they waited for the door to open.

“Now Enzo, did you actually see her or are you just guessing as usual?” She asked, directing her question to the man next to Tom. The man laughed,

“Come on Lilah, none of us would actually see her coming, she’s the fastest woman in the city. But I thought I saw a streak of red go past all the windows.”

“That’s exactly what you said a few minutes ago!” The woman responded. The two began arguing, and in the meantime Tom found his attention being drawn to the bar as a whole.
