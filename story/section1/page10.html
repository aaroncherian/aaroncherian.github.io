Section 1

“You know, even coming back here for us was bittersweet. From what I’ve heard we lost some good people to the job. Some people we knew are…not around anymore.” Lilah hesitated on the last few words, because while it was one thing to be familiar with death, as they all were, it was another entirely to be comfortable using the word easily.

“Not everyone made it,” Tom said.

“They served our city well,” Enzo murmured, before taking another bite of his food.

“They served our city well,” Lilah and Tom repeated, old habits still alive and well. Bryan mouthed the words with them.
