Section 1

“Seven,” Tom replied. A moment of silence passed over them.

“That long huh?” Lilah said quietly, before smiling and looking over at Tom. “Well thanks for setting this whole thing up Tom, I think I can speak for everyone when I say it’s good to be back.”

“You know, this place hasn’t changed a bit,” Tom said quietly.

“Still the same as ever,” Lilah agreed. “Loud. And chaotic.” She glanced over at Enzo as she said that last part.

“Well I for one, am glad,” Enzo declared. “I’m not sure about you guys, but I don’t feel like a stranger here at all. I kind of miss those days actually, coming here after a shift on patrol. It was nice to hang out with friends.”
