Section 1

Lilah followed his gaze.

“You know, the weather was just like this, the last time we were all together,” she mused. The others turned their head now to the window as well. Tom thought back to that last day they had been together, but the memory was slippery, sliding out of his reach as he tried to grasp it.

“Was it really? I don’t remember that,” he said. Thinking on the matter further, he frowned. He could remember the general time and place, but anything beyond that was hazy and distorted.

“Say guys, I'm blanking out all of a sudden. Why was that our last night together?” He asked.

Bryan gave him a strange look, and the others glanced at him in surprise.

“Tom, how do you forget that night, of all nights?” Ayano asked in what Tom thought might have been a disappointed tone.
