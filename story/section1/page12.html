Section 1

“The last time… we were all together,” Tom murmured.

Tom found his attempt to think back to that night foiled by the disconcerting fact that he just couldn’t remember it. The details were there, of that he was sure. He could feel them just at his fingertips, but the memories kept slipping away as he reached for them.

With a growing sense of panic, Tom realized there were quite a few things that had didn’t remember about the past seven years. Even his recollection of anything before the bar was clouded. Where had he been before he arrived here? What had all of them been doing over the past years?

Most importantly, what had driven them apart?
