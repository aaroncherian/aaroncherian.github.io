Section 1

Everyone seemed to be in good cheer, though the mood was rarely anything else. And by the very nature of its customers, the bar was constantly in state of chaos. Trays of drinks and food were floating through the air, filled ones moving away from the bartender, while she made another series of movements with her hand to pull empty ones off the various tables towards her.

Powers were often flying around at random, as heroes showed off their powers. One particular hero seemed to be stealing everyone's attention that night, rivulets of water flowing around her in intricate shapes. There was good natured cheering throughout the bar as she manipulated the ebb and flow of the liquid. Even after seven years, there was always one her who would steal the spotlight.
